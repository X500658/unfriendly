# Special Readings of Numbers

## Ages
```
(Number)さい
```
* 1 - `いっ`さい
* 8 - `はっ`さい
* 10 - `じゅっ`さい
* 20 - `はたち`

20 is the only special special case. 11, 18, 21, 30, etc. will be read following the special readings.

## Months
* April (4) - `し`がつ
* July (7) - `しち`がつ
* September (9) - `く`がつ
